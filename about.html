We think beyond

Lithion has an ambitious vision: to enable the world
to be powered by the greenest batteries available.

We want to do things better by offering better technologies, a better approach to market, and a better business model. We rethink the value chain of lithium-ion batteries for you, for us and above all, for our children.

This vision made possible today what was unthinkable in 2017: recycling 95% of battery materials while producing 75% less GHGs than existing solutions.

We’re aiming high. Our goal is for every major city to have its own Lithion critical mineral extraction plant to feed Lithion hydrometallurgy plants and supply the world with recycled strategic materials.

And we will get there! Beyond our cutting-edge technologies, we rely on a passionate team to offer the market the high-performance solutions it needs.

Our 5 values

  • What got us here won't get us there

    We don't see excellence as the final destination, but rather as a starting point. We set ourselves difficult goals to challenge ourselves and constantly strive for improvement. We innovate to enhance what is already working well and aim to bring positive changes. We thrive to innovate and find new ways to make things better, and this constant search for improvement is what makes us strong.

  • Determination in our eyes. Smile on our faces.

    We always try to step outside our comfort zone. We don’t shy away from difficult questions or tough challenges. A day without laughter? Impossible at Lithion. Working while having fun is one of the reasons we got here and why our future is bright. Even if we fully understand the urgency for battery circularity, we remain playful and never take ourselves too seriously.

  • Uncompromising on circularity

    We want to build a circular economy without going in circles. This means we are committed to evaluating the impact of all our decisions. We are proud that this is at the heart of all our thinking. As lifelong learners and straight talkers, we love clarity and openness. We are committed to being honest and transparent with our customers, colleagues, partners and stakeholders. Always.

  • Sharing is caring

    Partner or colleague – we love everyone. We are a team working together to bring out the best in each other We know that, day in and day out, we must act as a team, without silos and egos. Not always easy, but it pays off. That’s how we can progress as one and learn from our collective wisdom.
